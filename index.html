WE ARE CONSTRUCTION COMPANY

Making your vision come true at the basics.

Duta Klasik Sdn Bhd was incorporated in 2003 as a bumiputera firm specializing in mechanical and electrical contracting. Years of building a reputation as competent, reliable contractor and supplier created an opportunity to dig deeper as a company. In 2008, Duta Klasik commenced fabrication and rental of Positive Pressure Welding Enclosures (PPWE) @ Habitat for the oil & gas industry. That same year, Duta Klasik’s PPWE solutions earned the SIRIM-QAS International as well as the ISO 9001:2008 certification from Det Norske Veritas(DNV).

Meet Our CEO

Leading with Vision and Integrity

Our CEO, Dato’ Haji Rosdin Bin Dato’ Haji Mohd Yusup has been the CEO of Duta Klasik Sdn Bhd since July 2010. Upon graduating as a petroleum engineer in 1988, he joined Maju Kenso Sdn Bhd as a Sales Engineer prior to assuming a Senior Sales Engineer position at Antah Oil Tools Sdn Bhd in 1990. He further developed his technical skills when he joined FisherRosemount in 1997 as a Product Specialist for field instruments. Dato’ Haji Rosdin left the company in 2000 to join Sapura Energy Sdn Bhd as Sales Manager
